On the Subject of Determining Labeled Priorities' Variants

I don't even know who's on third for this!

Refer to Appendix: Label Priority List for the list of phrases that show up on Labeled Priorities and its variants.

The module contains 4 selectable displays. This page will go over the gimmicks for this module and its variants.

The defuser will need to determine what variant Labeled Priorities is by inspecting the what is currently shown on the screens.

If the screens:

  • All display unique phrases from the The Label Priority List, the module is Labeled Priorities.
  • All display single digits, the variant is Unlabeled Priorities.
  • All display the exact same phrase from the The Label Priority List, the variant is Relabeled Priorities.
  • Have one displaying a 4-digit number and the rest a unique phrase from the The Label Priority List, the variant is Mislabeled Priorities.

Once the variant is determined, the expert will need to go to the respective page and follow the instructions there to disarm the module. Failing to follow the specified instructions will result in a strike
